about me

Hi! :)

Welcome to a digital portfolio of my work!

My name is Kirsten Wijaya, i'm 18 20 years old, and currently a digital media student based in Melbourne, Australia.

On this website, you'll find a selection of work i've completed across various design areas, such as video editing work, coding, web and icon design, as well as 3D work in Unity. you'll be able to see an insight of my thought process behind the works and how i've come to produce the final product.

Personally, my favourite area of work is video editing and vfx work in After Effects. i've been editing as a hobby since i was about 14, and incorperating Adobe After Effects into my work since i was roughly 15.

Since then and throughout my highschool years, i've branched out to more mediums of digital media, such as Adobe Dreamweaver, Illustrator, Animate, Photoshop, and more recently; Brackets and Unity.

Apart from digital media, my other interests include but are not limited to; gaming, star wars, softball/baseball, drawing/art, and creative writing.
