statement and contexualisation

This work is a video piece which speculates on a fictional dystopian world.

The video, which takes the form of an advertisement, is set in a distant future where technology and AI have advanced greatly. The advert discusses a smart future city developed within the capitalist mindset. Chasing a skewed and arguably biased idea of perfection, the advertisement beckons viewers to come live in a city that essentially only fits a capitalist’s view of a utopia. The working scheme is hidden behind attractive technology and convenience, and aims to rope the working class citizen into essentially working to pay off their lives.

Similar to my other video works, this work was created entirely in After Effects. To create the artificial feeling of superficial and out-of-touch advertisements, I used a combination of stock footage combined with my own shot footage.

As someone primarily within the video effects, post production, and motion graphics area, I focused on the editing of my own footage. I colour graded, rotoscoped, motion tracked, pre-composed, masked and animated the videos to further push my work to portray my speculated fiction.

This video work was made as a speculation of an imagined future I see unfolding based on present day aspects of digital media.

Inspired by the movie Tron (2010), my speculation delves into the discussion and ideas surrounding the definition of perfection, and what a perfect world would encapsulate. While planning my video, I fleshed out the factor of perspective, and how perspective and experiences can heavily impact the way a person defines a “utopia”. With this in mind, I created what would be “perfection” in the eyes of a capitalist corporation or company, but a dystopia in the eyes of the average working person.
